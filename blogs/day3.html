🍂 Day 3 – Autumn Whispers

Day three was wrapped in soft breezes and falling leaves. 🍁

I spent the morning sketching quietly in my notebook, inspired by the shades of brown, gold, and rust around me. The crisp air felt refreshing, like nature’s gentle whisper.

Lunch was simple but comforting, and the afternoon was filled with writing letters — something rare these days but so heartwarming. ✉️

By evening, I went on a stroll, listening to the rustle of leaves under my steps. It felt like walking through poetry.

💭 Reflection

Autumn teaches me that letting go can be beautiful too. 🍂

🍂 Highlights of Day 3

  • ✏️ Quiet morning sketching
  • 🍽 Simple, comforting lunch
  • ✉️ Writing letters
  • 🍁 Evening stroll in autumn air
