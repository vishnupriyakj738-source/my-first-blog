🌿 Day 4 – Lavender Calm

Day four felt like a soft lullaby wrapped in lavender. 🌸

The morning began with meditation, breathing in calmness and breathing out worries. The quiet rhythm of my heart matched the serenity of the day.

I treated myself to a warm lavender tea ☕, its fragrance filling the air with peace.

In the evening, I spent time journaling and reflecting under soft candlelight 🕯, finding comfort in stillness.

💭 Reflection

Calmness isn’t found outside, but within — a quiet garden of peace. 🌿
