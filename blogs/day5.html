🌧 Day 5 – Raindrop Songs

Rain blessed my morning with its gentle rhythm against the windows. 🌧

I curled up with a blanket, sipping hot chocolate 🍫, while listening to raindrops tapping like a soft melody.

The afternoon turned cozy with homemade pakoras, laughter, and storytelling with family.

By evening, the rain slowed, leaving behind the fresh earthy scent of petrichor. 🌱

💭 Reflection

Rain reminds me to pause — even storms bring gentle music. 🌧

🌧 Highlights of Day 5

  • 🌧 Listening to raindrops
  • 🍫 Hot chocolate & cozy blankets
  • 🍴 Homemade pakoras
  • 🌱 Smell of fresh earth after rain

✨ "Some days, the rain is the only song you need." 🌧
